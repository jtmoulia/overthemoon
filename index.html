The Program

We're excited to celebrate with you! Come for what you can, stay for what you like.

May 22nd - Explore J. Tree

Explore Joshua Tree. Join us as we explore Joshua Tree National Park and the surrounding area. Email hello@pocketknife.io if you are interested in hiking, climbing, or exploring local art with other wedding guests.

May 23rd - Fun & Dinner

Explore Joshua Tree, and have dinner with us! We'll be out in the park again, exploring, hiking and climbing for the day. We're having an open "rehearsal dinner" cookout at a campground in the national park at 5pm. RSVP to hello@pocketknife.io if you can join us!

May 24th - Love and Marriage

Wedding! Hang out at you leisure in JTree all day, but join us at 5:30pm for the wedding ceremony at 8919 La Contenta Rd, Yucca Valley, CA 92284. Immediately following the ceremony we will be hosting a big reception on the same property. Join us for dancing, drinks, astronomy, and general merriment. (Plenty of space to sleep over at the venue, just email ahead).

The Who

Thomas is my climbing partner, my running buddy, my strange recipe tester, and my best friend. I was 45 minutes late for our first date, and he stayed, inexplicably, to meet me. He biked 60 miles to help my family dig out after a tornado. He matches me in every interest and urge, and I can't imagine a better person to adventure with for the rest of my life. Forget earth - I'm the luckiest woman in the galaxy.
