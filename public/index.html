The Program

We're excited to celebrate with you! Come for what you can, stay for what you like.

May 22nd - Explore J. Tree

Join us as we explore Joshua Tree National Park and the surrounding area. Email hello@pocketknife.io if you are interested in hiking, climbing, or exploring local art with other wedding guests.

May 23rd - Fun & Dinner

We'll be out in the park again, exploring, hiking and climbing for the day. We're having an open "rehearsal dinner" cookout at a campground in the national park at 5pm. RSVP to hello@pocketknife.io for more details if you can join us!

May 24th - Love and Marriage

Join us at 6pm for the wedding ceremony at 8919 La Contenta Rd, Yucca Valley, CA 92284. Immediately following the ceremony we will be hosting a big reception on the same property. Join us for dancing, drinks, astronomy, and feats of strength.
